My Journey as a Survivor.

As a survivor of abuse, I've learned that silence only perpetuates the pain. For too long, I suffered in silence, but today, I choose to speak out. Abuse doesn't discriminate; it can happen to anyone. I endured emotional manipulation, verbal assaults, and physical violence, but I refused to let it define me. With the support of a trusted friend, I found the courage to break free and rebuild my life. Though the scars remain, I'm no longer a victim—I'm a survivor, standing tall and sharing my story to inspire others to find their strength and break free from the cycle of abuse. Together, let's break the silence and empower survivors everywhere.
